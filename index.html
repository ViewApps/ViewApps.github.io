Numbers & Date Time Counter
2021/03/09

This js library is for counting numbers and dates, which counts numbers both individually and in groups, and dates as both decreasing and increasing. And in both cases, it is possible to determine the variety of digit in Persian and Latin.

  • number counting
  • group numbers counting
  • date counting down to ...
  • date counting up from ...
  • set callback function
  • persian or latin digit
